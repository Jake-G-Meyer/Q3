My Top 10 Favorite TV Shows

  1. Futurama

    2. Futurama is an animated sitcom about a guy named Fry who is accidently frozen for a thousand years and wakes up in the future where he goes on wild adventures with sarcastic space captain Leela and the rude, cigar smoking robot Bender.

  2. The Honeymooners

    3. The Honeymooners is a classic sitcom about bus driver Ralph Kramden, his wife Alice, and their friends/neighbors Ed and Trixie Norton.

  3. Married...With Children

    4. In the show that made the Fox channel famous, former high school football star Al Bundy is now a miserable father who goes through crazy misadventures his lazy wife Peggy, his dimwitted daughter Kelly, his loser son Bud, and his rival neighbor Marcy.

  4. All In The Family

    5. Regarded as one of the most revolutionary sitcoms of all time, this show follows the opiniative Archie Bunker and his kindhearted wife Edith, who live with their opiniative daughter Gloria and her voice of reason husband Mike.

  6. The Golden Girls

  7. The Dick Van Dyke Show
